## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Core Game Functionality

- **Authentication**: Verify your identity through Pi Network
- **Location Verification**: Confirm your presence at mining nodes
- **Reward Distribution**: Calculate and distribute Pi rewards fairly
- **Progress Tracking**: Maintain your game progress and achievements
- **Anti-Cheat Protection**: Prevent fraudulent activities and ensure fair play

### Service Improvement

- **Analytics**: Understand how players interact with the game (anonymized data only)
- **Bug Fixes**: Identify and resolve technical issues
- **Feature Development**: Develop new features based on usage patterns
- **Performance Optimization**: Improve app performance and user experience

### Communication

- **Game Updates**: Notify you about important game updates and features
- **Security Alerts**: Inform you about security-related matters
- **Community Events**: Share information about special events and competitions
